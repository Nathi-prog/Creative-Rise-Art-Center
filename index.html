About Creative Rise

A dynamic and innovative artistic hub committed to nurturing talent and fostering creative excellence in Lenasia South.

Our Mission

To empower young artists and community members through structured artistic programs that build skills, confidence, and creative expression.

Fostering innovation and artistic exploration

Our Vision

To transform Lenasia South into a thriving creative community where artistic talent is recognized, developed, and celebrated.

Creating cultural impact through the arts

Why Choose Creative Rise?

  • Qualified and passionate instructors dedicated to youth development
  • Safe and inspiring environment for creative exploration
  • Affordable programs accessible to all community members
  • After-school hours to accommodate students' schedules
  • Regular exhibitions and showcases for student work
